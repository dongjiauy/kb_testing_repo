## 1. Vision: From Robotaxi to Smart Logistics
- **Narrative Shift**: We are not just a Robotaxi company. We are building the **"Operating System for Urban Logistics"**.
- **Reason**: Robotaxi path is too long (5-10 years). Logistics is here (1-3 years). Cash flow positive by 2027.

## 2. Traction (The Good)
- **Logistics Partners**: Pilot running with SF Express (顺丰) in Hangzhou.
- **Mileage**: 1.2M km autonomous miles (L4).
- **Fleet**: 50 Robotaxis (Beijing/Guangzhou), 200 Logistics Vans (Hangzhou).

## 3. The Problem (The Bad)
- **Hardware Cost**: Currently ¥250k per van. Too high for mass adoption.
- **Goal**: Reduce BOM to ¥100k within 12 months.
- **Regulation**: Beijing license plates are frozen. Need to expand to Shenzhen.

## 4. Ask
- **Amount**: $100M USD.
- **Use of Funds**:
  - 40% Mass Production (Factory setup).
  - 30% R&D (Algorithm optimization).
  - 20% Operations (Field engineers).
  - 10% Policy/Lobbying.

## 5. Team
- **CEO (Li Zong)**: Ex-Baidu Apollo, Ex-Waymo.
- **CTO (Zhang Bo)**: PhD from Tsinghua, 15 years in Computer Vision.
- **COO (Old Zhou)**: Ex-DJI Supply Chain VP.
