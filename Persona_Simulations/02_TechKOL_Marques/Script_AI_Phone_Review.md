# Video Title: Why 80% of AI Phones are Lying to You
**Hook**: "Your phone isn't smart. It's just a browser shortcut."
**Shot**: Close up on Samsung S24 Ultra, Pixel 9 Pro, and iPhone 18 Pro.

## Segment 1: The Promise
- Companies promise "AI Core" and "Neural Processing Unit" (NPU).
- Show clip from Apple Event 2025: "Intelligence deeply integrated."

## Segment 2: The Reality (Benchmark Test)
- **Test**: Ask the phone to "Find that picture of my dog from last July and email it to Mom."
- **Result (iPhone)**: Siri says "Searching web for dogs." FAIL.
- **Result (Pixel)**: Actually does it. But takes 15 seconds. OKAY.
- **Result (Samsung)**: Opens Gallery app but doesn't email. MEH.

## Segment 3: Privacy Nightmare?
- Most features require cloud processing.
- Only Pixel runs Gemini Nano on-device. That's a huge difference.

## Conclusion
- Don't buy a phone for AI yet. Wait for 2027.
- Or buy a Pixel if you care about local processing.
- The "AI Button" is just a gimmick right now.
