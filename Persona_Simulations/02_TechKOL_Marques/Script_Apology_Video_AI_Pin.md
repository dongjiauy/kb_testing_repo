# Video Script: I Was Scammed. (And I'm Sorry.)
**Tone**: Serious, no music, black t-shirt.

## Intro
(Deep sigh)
"I messed up. I pride myself on only recommending tech that I actually believe in. But with the 'AI Pin', I let the hype cloud my judgment."

## The Facts
- I received a pre-production unit. It worked perfectly.
- The units you guys received... didn't. They overheat, they hallucinate, and the battery lasts 2 hours.
- It turns out the unit I had was running a custom firmware that connected to a server in a backpack (literally). It wasn't real.

## The Action
- I am delisting the review video.
- I am returning the sponsorship money ($50k) and donating it to the EFF (Electronic Frontier Foundation).
- I am suing the company for breach of contract and reputational damage.

## Conclusion
"Trust takes years to build and seconds to break. I hope you can forgive me. I will do better."
