# TikTok Pivot Strategy (Idea Board)

## Why?
- Long-form views are down 30% YoY.
- Gen Z doesn't watch 20min reviews anymore.
- I need to feed the algorithm beast.

## Concepts
1.  **"Tech Roast" (15s)**: Pick one bad feature and destroy it.
    - Example: The Cybertruck's panel gaps.
2.  **"Does it Suck?" (30s)**: Binary verdict. Buy or Pass.
    - Fast cuts. High energy. (I hate this style, but it works).
3.  **"ASMR Unboxing" (60s)**: No talking. Just peeling plastic.
    - Low effort, high views.

## Blocker
- I need to learn vertical framing. My $50k RED camera is useless here.
- Need to hire a Gen Z editor who understands "Skibidi" humor (God help me).
