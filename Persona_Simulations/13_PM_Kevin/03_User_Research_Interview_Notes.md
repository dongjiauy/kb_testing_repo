# User Interview Notes: "Why do you hate the new UI?"
**Participant**: User A (Accountant, 45y)
**Date**: 2026-03-22

## Key Feedback
- "Where is the 'Save' button? It used to be top right. Now it's hidden under a '...' menu? Who thought that was smart?"
- "The font is too grey. It looks modern but I can't read it."
- "I miss the Excel export. The new CSV export messes up the date formats."

## Kevin's Takeaway
- We over-designed it.
- Designers prioritized "Clean" over "Functional".
- **Action**: Bring back the explicit Save button in the next sprint.
