Title: Divorce Mediation (Session 1)
Date: 2026-03-12
Participants: Sarah, Husband (Tom), Mediator

Mediator: Tom, let's start with you. Why are we here?

Tom: We're here because I'm married to a ghost. I haven't seen Sarah for dinner in 6 months. I raise our daughter alone. I go to parent-teacher conferences alone.

Sarah: Tom, I save lives! Last night I was in the OR for 8 hours trying to save a 20-year-old kid who got stabbed. I can't just "clock out" at 5 PM.

Tom: I know, Sarah. You're a hero. Everyone says so. But I don't need a hero. I need a wife. I need a mother for Lily.

Sarah: I'm doing this for Lily! Private school costs $40k a year. Who pays for that?

Tom: I make money too! We don't need the private school if it means we never see you.

Sarah: You don't understand. The hospital is understaffed. If I leave, people die.

Tom: And if you stay, our marriage dies. It's already dead, actually.

Mediator: Okay, let's take a breath.
