# Review Comments: Paper ID 402 (ICLR 2027)
**Recommendation**: Reject

## Summary
The authors propose a "new" attention mechanism called "SuperAttention".

## Strengths
- Good theoretical analysis of complexity.

## Weaknesses
- **Lack of Novelty**: This is mathematically equivalent to "Linear Attention" proposed in 2020. The authors just renamed the variables.
- **Experiments**: Only tested on CIFAR-10. This is 2027! Please use ImageNet or LAION.

## Conclusion
The paper ignores prior art and the experiments are weak.
