# Legal Opinion: IP Risk in Nexus Deal
**Privileged & Confidential**

## Executive Summary
We have uncovered a significant risk regarding the target's "Entity Extraction" module.

## The Facts
- The core algorithm was written by a contractor (Rob) *before* he incorporated the company.
- There is no IP Assignment agreement from that period.
- Technically, Rob personally owns the code, not the company.

## The Risk
- If Rob gets hit by a bus (or divorced), his estate/spouse could claim ownership of Nexus AI's core tech.
- This is a "Deal Killer" level risk.

## Recommendation
- We must force Rob to sign a "Confirmatory Assignment" backdated to 2024.
- We need to hold back $5M in escrow until this is signed.
