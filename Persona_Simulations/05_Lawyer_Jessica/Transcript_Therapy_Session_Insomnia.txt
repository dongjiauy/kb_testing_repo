Title: Therapy Session - Insomnia
Date: 2026-03-13

Therapist: Jessica, you said you haven't slept in 3 days. What's keeping you up?

Jessica: The deal. It's always the deal. I close my eyes and I see redlines. I see missed commas that could cost my client millions.

Therapist: What happens if you miss a comma?

Jessica: I get sued for malpractice. I get disbarred. I become a barista.

Therapist: That sounds like catastrophic thinking. Has anyone ever actually sued you for a comma?

Jessica: No. But my boss yells. He throws files. Yesterday he called me at 2 AM to ask about a font size.

Therapist: Why did you pick up?

Jessica: Because if I don't, the associate next to me will. And he will make Partner, and I will be 35 and unemployed.

Therapist: You are defining your worth by your billing hours. We need to work on that.
