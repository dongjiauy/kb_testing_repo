# Final Decision Matrix: Stay vs Go

## Option A: Stay at Nexus AI
- **Pros**:
  - I built this. It's my baby.
  - Title (Principal/Architect) is higher than Google.
  - If we IPO, the equity could be worth $5M+.
- **Cons**:
  - Kevin (New VP) is toxic.
  - Li Zong (CEO) is pivoting every 2 months. Chaos.
  - On-call rotation is killing me. GlobalBank outage took 10 years off my life.
  - Current equity value: $0 (Paper money).

## Option B: Go to Google
- **Pros**:
  - Money. $500k+ TC guaranteed.
  - Stability. No 3am PagerDuty calls (hopefully).
  - Resume gold.
- **Cons**:
  - I'm just a cog in the machine.
  - L6 at Google is hard work. Politics.
  - Golden handcuffs.

## Decision
My heart says stay and fight Kevin. My brain (and my wife) says take the money.
I'll give Li Zong one last chance. If he backs Kevin on the Rust rewrite, I walk.
