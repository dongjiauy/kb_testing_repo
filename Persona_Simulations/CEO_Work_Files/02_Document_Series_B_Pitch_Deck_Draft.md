# Nexus AI - Series B Pitch Deck (Draft v4)

## Title Slide
**Nexus AI: Converting Enterprise Data into Active Intelligence**
- Raising: $30M Series B
- Valuation Target: $200M pre-money
- Lead Investor: TBD (Sequoia/Hillhouse interested)

## Slide 1: The Problem
- Enterprises have data silos (Slack, Jira, Salesforce, G-Drive).
- Search is broken. Employees spend 20% of time looking for files.
- Current AI wrappers are just "chatbots" – they don't have context.

## Slide 2: The Solution (Our Secret Sauce)
- **Active Knowledge Graph**: Not just RAG. We build a dynamic graph of entities.
- **Privacy-First Architecture**: We don't train on customer data. We fine-tune adapters.
- **Latency**: Sub-second query response (Note: Current bottleneck is being fixed, target <800ms).

## Slide 3: Traction (The Hockey Stick)
- **ARR**: $2.5M -> $8.1M (300% YoY Growth).
- **Clients**: 45 Enterprise Customers (Logo Wall: GlobalBank, TechCorp, PharmaInc).
- **Retention**: Net Revenue Retention (NRR) at 125%.
- **Churn**: <5% logo churn.

## Slide 4: Use Case - GlobalBank (Confidential)
- Problem: Compliance team took 4 days to audit loan documents.
- Solution: Deployed Nexus On-Prem.
- Result: Audit time reduced to 4 hours. 90% accuracy.
- ROI: Saved $2M/year in manual labor.

## FAQ Prep (Internal Notes)
- Q: "Why not just use ChatGPT Enterprise?"
- A: Data sovereignty. OpenAI owns the model; we give you the model. Plus, we integrate deeply into internal APIs, not just documents.
- Q: "What's your moat?"
- A: The proprietary graph construction algorithm. It gets smarter the more data sources you connect. LLMs are commodities; context is the king.
- Q: "Burn rate?"
- A: Currently $400k/mo. With Series B, we ramp to $1.2M/mo to capture market share. Runway extends to 24 months.
