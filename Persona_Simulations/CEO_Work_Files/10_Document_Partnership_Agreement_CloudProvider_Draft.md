# Strategic Partnership Agreement - Draft v1.2
**Party A**: Nexus AI, Inc. ("Nexus")
**Party B**: TechCorp Cloud Solutions ("TechCloud")

## 1. Objective
To jointly market and deploy the "Active Knowledge Graph" solution on TechCloud's infrastructure to Enterprise clients.

## 2. Terms of Collaboration
- **Exclusivity**: Nexus agrees to recommend TechCloud as the preferred deployment partner for on-premise installations in the APAC region.
- **Revenue Share**: TechCloud receives 20% of the recurring software license fee for deals sourced by their sales team. Nexus receives 10% of the compute revenue generated by the deployment.
- **Co-Marketing**: Joint budget of $500,000 for events (AWS re:Invent booth, etc.).

## 3. Product Integration
- Nexus will optimize its vector database engine for TechCloud's proprietary "T-Chip" accelerators.
- TechCloud will provide $100,000 in free compute credits for R&D testing.

## 4. Legal & IP
- **Intellectual Property**: Nexus retains all rights to its algorithms and models. TechCloud retains all rights to its hardware optimizations.
- **Data Privacy**: TechCloud agrees to comply with GDPR/CCPA. No customer data will be accessed by TechCloud personnel.

## 5. Termination
- Either party may terminate with 90 days notice.
- Revenue share obligations survive termination for 12 months for existing customers.
