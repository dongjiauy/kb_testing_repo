# Q1 2026 OKR Final Review

## Objective 1: Achieve Product-Market Fit in Enterprise
- **KR1: Close 5 paid pilots with Fortune 500 companies.**
  - **Result: 3 Closed.** (GlobalBank, TechCorp, PharmaInc)
  - **Gap**: Missed target due to long procurement cycles.
  - **Learning**: Need to simplify legal review (hence "Vault" architecture).
- **KR2: Reduce Onboarding Time from 4 weeks to 1 week.**
  - **Result: 1.5 Weeks.**
  - **Gap**: Data connector integration (Salesforce API) is tricky.
  - **Action**: Build custom connector for Salesforce (Hiring specialist).

## Objective 2: Scale Revenue (The "Fuel")
- **KR1: Reach $2.5M ARR Run Rate.**
  - **Result: $2.8M ARR.** (EXCEEDED by +12%)
  - **Driver**: Unexpected upsell from TechCorp (expanded to 500 seats).
  - **Risk**: 60% of revenue comes from 2 clients. High concentration risk.

## Objective 3: Team Excellence (Culture)
- **KR1: Hire 5 Senior Engineers (L5+).**
  - **Result: 2 Hired.**
  - **Gap**: Market for NLP talent is insane. Comp packages are exploding.
  - **Action**: Increase equity grant pool by 20%.

## New North Star Metric for Q2: **Retention Rate (NRR)**
- Why? We have enough leads. We need to keep them happy.
- **Target**: 120% NRR.
- **Critical Path**: Fix Search Latency (<800ms) & Launch "Meeting Assistant".

## CEO Commentary
Q1 was a mixed bag. Revenue is great, but product stability is shaky. We are growing faster than our infrastructure can handle. Q2 is about **Optimization**. Not new features (except Meeting Assistant), but making existing features bulletproof. Let's not lose our soul in the chase for growth.

-- Alex
