# Strategy Retreat 2026 - Brainstorming (Personal Memo)

## 1. Organization Structure
- **Current Problem**: Too centralized. Product team (Dave/Sarah) is getting bogged down. Sales is disconnected from R&D.
- **Solution Idea**: "Amoeba" Model (Kyocera Style)?
    - Split into mini-startups: "Core Search", "Analytics", "Integrations".
    - Each unit has its own P&L (Profit & Loss).
    - But creates internal competition. Is that bad? Maybe good for speed?
    - Need to discuss with HR (Lisa). She will hate this.

## 2. Expansion - Japan Market
- **Pro**: Huge demand for enterprise efficiency. High ARPPU (Average Revenue Per User).
- **Con**: Culture barrier. Need a strong local partner (SoftBank? NTT?).
- **Competitors**: None strong yet. Microsoft is slow there.
- **Decision**: Start hiring a GM for Japan in Q2. Budget $2M for year 1.
    - If it works: $10M ARR potential in 18 months.
    - If it fails: $2M sunk cost. Acceptable risk.

## 3. Product Vision - "Nexus Agent"
- Stop building just "Search". Build "Action".
- The AI shouldn't just find the file; it should draft the reply based on the file.
- **Killer Feature**: "Meeting Assistant" that joins Zoom calls, transcribes, extracts action items, creates Jira tickets, and emails summary to participants. automatically.
- **Timeline**: Prototype by Q3. Launch by Q4.
