# Company Survey Results: "Return to Office" Sentiment
**Respondents**: 450 (90% participation)

## Key Findings
1.  **Sentiment**: 70% Negative, 20% Neutral, 10% Positive.
2.  **Top Concerns**:
    - Commute time (Average 1.5 hours/day).
    - Loss of focus (Open office noise).
    - Covid/Flu risk.

## Selected Comments
- "If I have to commute 2 hours just to sit on Zoom calls, I will quit."
- "The office coffee sucks."
- "I miss seeing my colleagues, but 2 days is enough. 5 days is prison."

## Recommendation
Proceed with "3+2" model. Do NOT attempt "5+0".
