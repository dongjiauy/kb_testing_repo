Title: Podcast Script - "Design Matters" Guest Spot
Host: Debbie
Guest: Chloe

Debbie: So Chloe, what is your design philosophy?

Chloe: I believe in "Less is More, but Warmth is Essential". Minimalism shouldn't be cold. It should be a canvas for life.

Debbie: How do you deal with difficult clients?

Chloe: (Laughs) I drink a lot of wine. No, seriously... I try to educate them. They don't know what they want until you show them.

Debbie: That's very Steve Jobs.

Chloe: I wish I had his budget!
